About Reddate 关于瑞枣

Technology that feels invisible. Care that never stops. 无感科技，不间断的呵护。

Founded by a team with deep expertise in IoT engineering and AI research, Reddate is built on academic rigour and real-world empathy. Our CTO holds a PhD in IoT systems, with research lineage in non-camera-based human detection. 瑞枣由物联网工程与AI研究领域的顶尖专家创立，兼具学术严谨与现实呵护。我们的CTO拥有物联网系统博士学位，专注于无摄像头人体检测技术的研究，这正是我们核心呵护功能的技术基石。

Our Mission我们的使命

To make every home a source of genuine care — for its residents, their families, and the moments that matter most. 让每一个家，都成为真正呵护的源泉——为居住者、为家人、为那些最重要的瞬间。

Our Vision我们的愿景

A world where your home knows you — not through surveillance, but through intelligence. Where care is continuous, quiet, and designed to be beautiful. 一个你的家真正了解你的世界——不靠监控，而靠智慧。呵护持续、静默，且美好。

The Platform核心平台

Not a device. An AI platform that learns your home. 不是设备，而是学习你的家的AI平台。

Reddate operates on three interconnected layers — each building on the last to deliver intelligent, continuous care without any cameras or active management. 瑞枣通过三个相互关联的层次运作——层层递进，无需摄像头与主动管理，持续提供智能呵护。

01

Sense感知

A network of non-camera sensors distributed through the home, capturing motion, temperature, air quality, energy consumption, sound patterns, and more. Always on. Always private. 非摄像头传感器网络，实时采集运动、温度、空气质量、能耗、声音模式等数据。全天候在线，始终守护隐私。

02

Learn学习

Our cloud AI builds a deep understanding of your home's unique patterns — who is there, when, and what is normal. It improves every single day. 云端AI深度理解你的家的专属规律——谁在家、何时在、何为正常。每一天都在进步，每一天都更了解你。

03

Care呵护

Based on what it knows, Reddate acts: alerting, adjusting, reporting, and protecting — automatically, silently, continuously. The longer it lives in your home, the smarter it becomes. 基于所学，瑞枣主动行动：预警、调节、报告、保护——自动、静默、持续。住得越久，它越智慧。
