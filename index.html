Core Philosophy

核心理念

Protect
without
watching. 守护，
不监视。

In an era of growing surveillance concerns, Reddate's no-camera architecture is a permanent competitive advantage. Behaviour is inferred from physics — radar, sound, pressure — not pixels. 在监控争议日益加剧的时代，瑞早的无摄像头架构是持久的竞争优势。行为通过物理信号推断——雷达、声音、压力——而非像素图像。

No images. No faces. No footage.

无图像，无面孔，无录像

Radar and acoustic sensors identify behavioural patterns from motion and sound waves alone. Nothing is ever recorded visually.

雷达和声学传感器仅通过运动和声波识别行为模式，从不进行任何视觉录制。

Systematic cross-validation — built to be right.

系统性交叉验证，专为精准而生

A platform where every sensor verifies every other sensor. Simple, user-friendly, and precise. This mutual verification is Reddate's core.

一个每个传感器相互印证的平台。简单、好上手、精准。这种相互验证是瑞早的核心所在。

Data stays where it belongs — in the home.

数据留在它该在的地方——家中

Sensitive behavioural data is processed locally at the edge. Only anonymised event summaries leave the premises.

敏感行为数据在本地边缘处理，只有匿名化的事件摘要才会离开家庭网络。

Care Scenarios

照护场景

Every risk. Every space. Covered. 每一种风险，每一个空间，全覆盖

Reddate's core research focus: multi-sensor linkage with dynamic tracking across all scenarios — precisely identifying the care subject in complex, real-world environments without error. Engineered for the hardest problems first. 瑞早核心研究方向：多场景联动全部传感器，联动追踪，在复杂场景精准识别照护对象不会出错。专门研究复杂场景，未来拓展到多领域。

Fall Detection
跌倒检测

Fall Monitoring & Pre-Fall Warning

摔倒监测与可能摔倒的预警

Instant detection via radar and acoustic sensors. Pre-fall risk scoring analyses gait unsteadiness before a fall occurs — enabling genuinely preventive care, not just reactive response.

雷达与声学传感器即时检测跌倒。跌倒前风险评分分析步态不稳，在事故发生前介入——实现真正的预防性照护，而非被动响应。

Location & Presence
动态跟踪与存在感知

Dynamic Tracking & Room Presence

动态跟踪、房间存在状况、离床警告

Real-time multi-room tracking identifies who is where and for how long. Triggers absence alerts, wandering detection, and bed-exit notification — across complex multi-person environments.

实时多房间追踪，识别人员位置与停留时长。触发离开警报、游走检测和离床通知——在复杂多人环境中精准识别照护对象。

Activity Intelligence
活动监测与分析

Activity Monitoring & Risk Forecast

持续活动能力监测与危险前兆分析

Continuous tracking of daily activity levels and mobility patterns. Declining activity is a key early warning sign — Reddate surfaces it weeks before clinical symptoms emerge.

持续追踪日常活动水平和运动模式。活动能力下降是关键早期预警信号——瑞早在临床症状出现前数周就识别危险前兆。

Vital Signs
生命体征

Heart Rate & Breathing Monitoring

老人心跳与呼吸监测

Non-contact radar continuously monitors respiratory rate and heartbeat without any wearable device — detecting arrhythmia, sleep apnoea, and sudden deterioration in real time.

非接触式雷达持续监测呼吸频率和心跳，无需任何可穿戴设备——实时检测心律不齐、睡眠呼吸暂停和突发恶化。

Bathroom Safety
卫生间安全

Bathroom Usage & AI Anomaly Alert

卫生间使用频率与AI分析异常预警

The highest-risk space. Sensors unaffected by steam monitor duration, frequency, and movement — alerting to falls, prolonged stays, or abnormal patterns that signal underlying health changes.

家中风险最高的空间。不受水汽影响的传感器监测如厕时长、频率和动作——对跌倒、超时停留或预示健康变化的异常模式发出AI预警。

Sleep & Bed Monitoring
睡眠与床位监测

Bed-Exit Alert & Sleep Quality Analysis

离床警告与睡眠质量分析

Mattress pressure sensors track posture, restlessness, and bed-exit events. AI correlates overnight patterns with long-term health trends — turning sleep data into meaningful health signals.

床垫压力传感器、脚垫压力传感器追踪睡眠姿势、躁动和离床事件。AI将夜间模式与长期健康趋势关联分析，将睡眠数据转化为有意义的健康信号。

All sensors are designed to work independently of light, steam, or obstructions. In challenging spaces like bathrooms and dark bedrooms, the system performs at full accuracy where cameras cannot operate at all. 所有传感器均不受光线、水汽或遮挡物的影响。在浴室和暗室等复杂空间，系统以完整精度运行——而摄像头在这些场所根本无法工作。

Multi-Sensor Cross-Validation

多传感器交叉验证

No single sensor makes a decision. Each sensor validates the others — eliminating false positives and ensuring precision in the most complex real-world scenarios. This mutual verification architecture is Reddate's core research breakthrough.

没有单一传感器独自决策。每个传感器相互验证——消除误报，确保在最复杂的现实场景中保持精准。这种相互验证架构是瑞早的核心研究突破。

Edge-First Processing

边缘优先处理

Sensitive data is processed locally on-device. Only anonymised event summaries leave the home — minimising latency and cloud exposure while meeting the strictest data sovereignty requirements.

敏感数据在设备本地处理，只有匿名化的事件摘要才会离开家庭——最大限度降低延迟和云端暴露，同时满足最严格的数据主权要求。

Personalised AI Baseline per Individual

个人化AI基线建模

Every resident's normal is different. The AI continuously models each individual's unique patterns — reducing false alarms and detecting meaningful deviation earlier and more accurately than any threshold-based system.

每个人的"正常"都不同。AI持续建模每位住户的独特规律——比任何阈值型系统更早、更准确地减少误报并检测有意义的偏差。

AI Intelligence

01

Unified Platform, AI-Ranked Alerts

统一平台，AI危险优先级梳理

All sensor data flows into a single platform. AI analyses across all inputs simultaneously and presents alerts ranked by danger level — not a list of raw events, but an intelligently triaged picture of what needs attention first.

所有传感器数据汇入一个平台。AI同时分析所有输入，按危险程度呈现警报——不是原始事件列表，而是经过智能分级的照护优先级图景。

02

Personalised Baseline — Fewer False Alarms

个性化基线——更少误报

Because the AI knows each individual's normal patterns, it only flags genuine anomalies. A night-owl staying up late is not an alert. An unusual stillness in the bathroom at 3am is.

因为AI了解每个人的正常规律，它只标记真正的异常。习惯熬夜的人深夜活动不是警报，但凌晨3点浴室出现的异常静止就是。

03

Predictive Health Signals — Weeks Ahead

提前数周的预测性健康信号

Declining mobility, shorter activity bouts, changing bathroom frequency — these are early warning signs. Reddate's AI surfaces these trends weeks before clinical deterioration becomes apparent.

行动能力下降、活动时长缩短、如厕频率变化——这些是早期预警信号。瑞早AI在临床恶化变得明显前数周就识别这些趋势。

04

Cross-Sensor Verification — Built to Be Right

跨传感器验证——专为精准而生

No alert fires from a single sensor. Every event is cross-referenced across the full sensor network before classification. This systematic mutual verification is what makes Reddate's platform simple, user-friendly, and precise.

没有警报由单一传感器触发。每个事件在分类前都经过全传感器网络交叉验证。这种系统性相互验证，让瑞早平台简单、好上手、精准。
